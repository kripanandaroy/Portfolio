How It Works

Application Process

Follow these simple steps to complete your application smoothly. We've designed the process to be quick, transparent, and hassle-free.

Step 01

Create Your Account

Sign up using your email address or social login. Fill in your basic profile information to get started. Make sure all details are accurate to avoid any delays.

Note: Use a valid, active email address — all communications will be sent there.

Email Google GitHub
Step 02

Submit Your Documents

Upload the required documents, such as your ID proof, academic transcripts, and any supporting certificates. Ensure all files are in PDF or JPG format and under 5MB each.

Note: Incomplete or blurry documents may lead to application rejection.

ID Proof Transcripts Certificates
Step 03

Application Review

Our team will carefully review your submitted application and documents. This process typically takes 3–5 business days. You will receive updates via email at each stage.

Note: Do not submit multiple applications — it may cause delays or disqualification.

3–5 Days Email Updates
Step 04

Get Your Decision

Once reviewed, you'll receive a final decision letter. If approved, follow the onboarding instructions provided. If not selected, you're welcome to reapply in the next cycle.

Tip: Check your spam folder if you haven't received a response after 5 business days.

Approved Re-apply Option
